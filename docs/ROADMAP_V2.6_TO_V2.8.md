# Product Roadmap — v2.6 to v2.8

## v2.6 — Unified Dashboard (completed)

- Provider-neutral Today experience
- Daily Readiness hero
- Recovery / Load / Energy pillars
- Unified Health Snapshot
- 7-day sparklines
- Health & Recovery drill-down
- 7-day and 28-day charts
- Explainable Coach Insight
- Data source details moved out of the main experience

## v2.7 — Personal Trends & Baselines

- 7 / 28 / 90 day trend controls
- Robust personal baseline bands
- Sleep debt and sleep consistency
- HRV and Resting HR deviation history
- Fitness / Fatigue / Form model
- Metric-specific detail screens
- Data coverage and confidence history
- Correlation views that clearly state association, not causation

## v2.8 — Trail Coach Intelligence

- Daily training prescription by readiness and planned session
- Elevation-aware and downhill-aware load progression
- Long-run and back-to-back readiness
- Weekly distance and vertical ramp controls
- Race readiness by distance, vertical, long-run duration and consistency
- Adaptive plan recommendations with explicit reasons and user approval
- Action tracking to compare recommendation versus completed session

## Governance

- No score may hide its contributors.
- Missing data must reduce confidence rather than be imputed silently.
- Pain safety gates override performance recommendations.
- Provider records remain traceable and deduplicated in the data layer.
- Each phase requires automated tests and a sync-safety report.
