# Personal Trends & Baselines — v2.7.0

## Purpose

Turn daily health and training records into athlete-specific trends without organizing the main experience by data provider.

## Included

- 7, 28 and 90 day views
- Rolling personal baseline and descriptive baseline band for Sleep, Resting HR and HRV
- Latest deviation from baseline in value and percentage terms
- Estimated seven-day sleep debt using only nights with recorded data
- Fitness, Fatigue and Form from transparent 42-day and 7-day exponential load averages
- Data-confidence indicator based on health coverage, baseline depth and training-load history

## Interpretation guardrails

- Baseline bands are personal descriptive ranges, not clinical reference ranges.
- Fitness, Fatigue and Form describe training-load balance and do not predict injury.
- Missing days remain missing and are not imputed as zero health values.
- Provider metadata remains available in Data & Sync for audit and troubleshooting only.
- Database schema, Worker code, tokens and provider sync logic are unchanged in this phase.
